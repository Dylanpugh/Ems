EMERGENCY TRANSPORT

Trinity EMS transports patients to and from Hospitals upon Emergency Contact. Our 24/7 dispatch systems are available with well-trained and experienced Certified EMT's on call for immediate response. Our ambulances are fully equipped with the latest Advanced Life Support and Critical Care tools and equipment.

NON-EMERGENCY TRANSPORT

Trinity EMS welcomes most non-emergency transports for those that need extra care to their destinations. From delivering a patient to their doctor appointments to taking that special someone to see their grandchild's first ballgame, we are there to serve.
